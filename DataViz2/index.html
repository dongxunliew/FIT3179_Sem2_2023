How does Happiness affect ...

It is generally believed that the happier a country is, the better the country is. But is this true?
Here, we compare each countries' happiness score with many other various facotrs.
The green dotted line represents the average happiness score of all countries,
while the blue dotted line represents the average of the factor being compared.
Click on the legend to filter the countries by region.

Generally, we can see that the happiness score of a country affects the other factors.
For GDP per Capita, Social Support, Healthy Life Expectancy, and Freedom to make life choices, they have a positive correlation with Happiness Score.
However, it is interesting that the same could not be said for Generosity and Perception of Corruption.
